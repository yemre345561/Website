General

-What is PRIOR and how does it work?

PRIOR is a world of curious travelers who seek cultural immersion and connection through wonder-filled experiences across the globe.

We approach travel with conviction, creating extraordinary experiences and editorial that celebrate and conserve what is beautiful, endemic and rare in world cultures.

Crafted by our editors and designers on the ground in destinations around the world, our Trips and Journeys go deep into cultures, traditions, crafts, flavors and natural environments for conscious and considered encounters, shaping rare and indelible moments with fellow travelers. Our PRIOR World Guides and Editorial newsletters inspire subscribers and members to dream and plan their travel with us. And our Custom Travel brings PRIOR Bespoke Members one-of-a-kind itineraries that combine their interests with access to our world.

-What are the guides?

PRIOR's highly-edited guides to our favorite destinations offer inside knowledge to the most stylish riads in Marrakech, the local wine bars in Venice, the most vibrant festivals in Spain, and the lush gardens of New Orleans.

Prior Guides are comprehensive digital guides to specific cities and regions around the world. They cover where to stay, what to eat, what to do, what to see, and where to shop. They also speak to the unique aspects of a place and showcase three feature articles that go into specific characteristics of the city and its culture that make it remarkable. Places include: Seville, New Orleans, Oaxaca, Venice, French Riviera, Marrakech, Napa and Sonoma, Savannah, Charleston.

Two original and highly authoritative weekly newsletters: PRIOR Wire, a discerning and digestible survey of travel news, trends, style, destinations and unbiased opinion; PRIOR Knowledge brings a world of wonder through its in-depth features, beautiful photography and thoughtful ideas about where to go and what to do, across the map.

Custom Travel

-What is Custom Travel and how does it work?

Working collaboratively with you, our destination experts design complete itineraries from A to Z that are tailored to your interests through our network. We create trips that incorporate a mix of places and experiences both high and low, humble and refined, that support our mission of cultural and environmental stewardship, resulting in truly singular itineraries.

Our pricing accounts for our time developing the trip and the insider access we provide in the quality of our experiences and accommodations. There are also many non-tangible items that go into the creation of a Custom PRIOR trip. We can obtain industry rates not accessible to the public, early check-ins (at selected hotels), upgrades where possible and spa credits at hundreds of hotels around the world. Our margin is already included in the final selling price of the proposal.

A $500 planning fee will be applied to every trip. There is a $1,000 per day minimum spend for Custom Planning, which includes lodging, transfers and experiences.

To learn more about our Custom planning terms, see our Terms and Conditions. You can also get in touch with our team directly.

-What is the cancellation policy for Custom Travel?

Cancellations are subject to the cancellation policies below, in addition to administrative fees related to Bespoke trip planning. Refunds and travel credits are subject to the policies of our global partners.

Cancellation charges are calculated as a percentage of your total trip price based on when the notice is received.

At time of booking, 10% non-refundable fee for planning and services.

Within 60 days of first scheduled date on confirmed itinerary, 50%

Within 45 days, 75%

Within 30 days, 90%

Within 15 days, 100%

Trips and Journeys

-What are PRIOR Trips and Journeys?

PRIOR’s calendar of Trips and Journeys offer multi-day celebratory gatherings in notable properties—such as an historic sherpa house in Nepal—as well as multi-destination explorations led by our extensive family of locals and experts who grant you insider access. On our Trips and Journeys you travel alongside like-minded, curious people growing your community and network. These trips have been designed by our team of experts in the worlds of food, art, craft, design and more. We bring our unique perspectives to create a world of wonder that touches the essence of the place you are visiting.To explore our calendar, click here.

-Who are these trips and journeys suitable for?

Our group trips are open to all! PRIOR members and non members, subscribers and non subscribers. They are typically geared towards adult interests and stamina. If you have a specific request, contact grouptrips@priorworld.com.

-Does booking one of the PRIOR Trips and Journeys include a membership to your travel club?

Our group trips are open to all and do not include a membership to our travel club. To learn more about a PRIOR membership, please contact membership@priorworld.com.

-Does booking one of the PRIOR Trips and Journeys include an editorial subscription to PRIOR online content?

Yes! Booking any of the PRIOR Trips and Journeys entitles you to a complimentary one year PRIOR Subscription, with access to our expert guides and editorial.

-What is included?

Our group trips generally include all accommodations, transportation, and tours as well as most meals and gratuities throughout the itinerary.

-What is not included?

Our group trips generally do not include airfare, visas (if applicable), travel insurance, personal expenses, certain meals and alcoholic beverages.

-Do you include or arrange international flights?

International airfare is not included. PRIOR members wishing to request help with flights, please contact travelteam@priorworld.com For more information about our membership program and its travel perks, please contact membership@priorworld.com

-I want to plan a pre or post trip extension, will you plan this?

Our in-house expert travel designers curate trips for PRIOR members only. To learn more about membership please contact membership@priorworld.com

-Do I need a passport?

Yes, a passport with at least 6 months validity after your trip ends and enough blank visa pages for entry and exit stamps from each country you visit.

-Can you accommodate my food allergies and/or restrictions?

Of course! Please list any food allergy or restriction on your guest form and we will ensure appropriate care and options are provided during your trip.

-Can we choose our rooms?

Rooming configurations are specific to each property. Please let our Membership team know of any requirements and/or to inquire about any upgrades. grouptrips@priorworld.com

-Can I amend or modify the trip itinerary?

No, given the nature of the group trips, modifications to the itinerary are not permitted.

-Can I arrive late or leave early?

Depending on the destination and trip, we will do our best to accommodate your arrival and/or departure. Please reach out to grouptrips@priorworld.com as soon as possible to coordinate.

-How do I guarantee my space?

To secure your space, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required. Bookings can be made online by following the Pay Deposit link on each trip page.

-Once I reserve my space what can I expect?

Once your space is reserved with a deposit, you will receive a welcome email with an itinerary overview and essential information details to start preparing you for the trip such as any visa requirements or vaccination requirements. A detailed, day by day itinerary along with a packing list will be sent two months prior to departure and your final itinerary with any trip updates will be sent two weeks before the trip.

-What is your payment and cancellation policy?

All payments are non refundable. For specific terms and conditions, please refer to your designated trip page for more details. In the event that PRIOR cancels or changes the dates of your departure, you will be entitled to a full refund.

-Can I use my hotel/ airline/ credit card points to pay?

No, only payment by credit card or wire transfer is accepted.

-What happens if I am unable to travel after purchasing due to travel or health restrictions?

Please let us know of any changes in your travel plans as soon as possible. We strongly recommend that all guests purchase comprehensive travel insurance before their trip.

-Are PRIOR Trips and Journeys suitable for children?

Most of our Trips and Journeys are currently geared towards adult interests and stamina, but if you have a specific request, contact grouptrips@priorworld.com.

-Who can I reach out to if I have more questions?

For any additional information please email us at grouptrips@priorworld.com
